About Me

I am a third-year undergraduate student at the Memorial University of Newfoundland pursuing my major in Computer Science and minor in Applied Mathematics. I had previously earned a Bachelor of Dental Surgery degree from India with distinction. Having been in love with computers since a very young age, my passion and eagerness to explore how technology works at different levels finally determined my choice for Computer Science. I am particularly interested in real-world projects; thus, networks and applications interest me the most.

I am currently working as a Business Development intern with “EcoBloc” through Experience Venture Program offered by Memorial University. I have held the position of Vice-President at the Indian Youth Association MUN and am currently the Science Society Representative with the Computer Science Society MUN. It has allowed me to hone my time-management skills and demonstrate organizational skills. Learning, along with the problem-solving aspect, seems rewarding to me. I am independently motivated and result-driven. I appreciate team effort and collaborate productively within groups.
